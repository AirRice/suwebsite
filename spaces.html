Useful spaces

You can get a lot of things done at the SU Building. Those are the most important facilities, at least in our opinion.

SSiD

It is a information service for students. They can get help with general inquiries and issues such as bank documents, problems with their U-card and other problems they may face. Their shop at SU Building provides Whereas students would normally need to visit the appropriate department to get help, the SSiD can help greatly by being a mediator. This allows students to visit the SSiD to get help no matter what the problem is related to.
Official website

Student Advice Centre

It is a professional, confidential service, providing advice and support to resolve students’ issues. The key areas they can help are: housing problems, employment, money and legal issues, mental health issues as well as many others. Compared to the SSiD they provide support for more delicate and personal issues.
Official website

JobShop

It is part of Careers Service provided by the University. There you can find part-time as well as full-time job offers for both current students and graduates. They help with writing CVs, cover letters and preparing for an interview, which maximises student’s chances for getting employed.
Official website

Study Spaces

There are many spots in the SU building to sit down and take a rest, and there are also meeting rooms that can be used in the Gallery area. In particular, during exam seasons the SU provides dedicated revision spaces throught the building.
