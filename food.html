Food and drink

There are lots of options to choose from. In fact, there are so many of them we haven't visited them all yet. Below you can find the list of our favourites places to eat and drink at SU Building.

coffee revolution

The Coffee Revolution shop offers ethically sourced coffee in addition to teas, smoothies, pastries, sandwiches, and other simple eats. It is located on Level 3 of the building, right near the main entrance.

Bar One

Bar One is the main bar of the student union. It provides several different kinds of beverages both alcoholic and non-alcoholic. Bar One also provides burgers and several kinds of events such as quizzes. The bar is on the lowest level of the building and is also accessible directly from Glossop Road.

Interval

The Interval Cafe & Bar serves a selection of wines and continental beers along with varied cuisine including vegetarian/vegan and gluten free menus. It is one of the more sophisticated places to eat in the building. The Interval is located on Level 2, opposite the Graves entrance.

New Leaf

The New Leaf is a salad bar providing fresh and healthy food for students and staff alike. You can create your very own takeaway salad or wrap by choosing which ingredients you would like, as the bar provides many different products.

Our Shop & Our Zero Waste Shop

Our Shop is a supermarket located in the centre of level 3. Here you can buy several different kinds of food including international products for those who feel homesick, and freshly baked pastries and sandwiches. The shop also contains a selection of stationery so you can stock up on study supplies as well.
The Zero Waste Shop sells a variety of different herbs and spices, dried nuts and fruits, and fragrances. You can buy these by weight, as much or little as you need. One can also refill household supplies such as detergent, hand soap and cleaning fluids here. The shop mostly strives to reduce waste and promote sustainability.

Proper Pastry Company

This store sells handmade pastries and some other foods such as jacket potatoes, slices of pizza, etc. It is a good place to buy small morsels of food on the go, as it is located in a convenient area (level 3, opposite Santander).

View Deli

The View Deli is a completely vegetarian and vegan cafe, located opposite the Uni Central. It redesigned its menu to cater to vegans and vegetarians in 2015.

You can check out more places to eat and drink at the official Students' website: https://su.sheffield.ac.uk/eat-drink-shop.
